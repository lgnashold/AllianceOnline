Welcome to Alliace Online, an multiplayer strategy game. Carefully craft alliances, joining teams so that you reap the benefits from their territory while conquering other player's lands. However, peace can not last for long, because if one player falls, alliances will break. It is your goal to be the last empire standing.

To make a game, type in a join code. If your friends type in the same code, you will all be put into the same game!
